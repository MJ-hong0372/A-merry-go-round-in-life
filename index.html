Where is Wolgye-dong Cathedral

The Wolgye-dong Cathedral was founded in 1992. The cathedral’s three bell towers represent the Christian faith of the Trinity. Above the crucifix is ​​a stained glass dove symbolizing the Holy Spirit.

I started attending this church in September 2023. It’s been 20 years since I’ve been to the church. The opportunity to believe in God comes by chance. I found great comfort in this church that I happened to visit, and I’m happy thanks to the many people and good priests.
I am the vice-director of the choir. Since there are only four members in the choir, I also serve as a secretary. Although I am not good at singing, I enjoy and feel proud of serving during mass.

My Dream

My Dream

My dream is to be a picture book author. I want to become an author who creates both pictures and text. I majored in Visual Information Design, but my graduation project was a children's book. I am very interested in children's childhood.
I believe that a person's childhood determines their entire life. I want to create therapeutic picture books for abused and suffering children, not just ordinary picture books. I won't deny that I had this purpose because my childhood was not happy. Sometimes, suffering can be the material for great creations.
Another dream is the happiness of our family. For the first time since I was born, I have a warm family. I have experienced how precious an ordinary life is. I want to do my best to maintain this happiness.
These two are my dreams.
